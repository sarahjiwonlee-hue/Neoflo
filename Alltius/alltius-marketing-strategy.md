## Suggested Messaging

### New Positioning Statement
> "Alltius builds AI agents that don't just answer questions — they execute. For financial services teams drowning in manual workflows, Alltius automates the work that was never meant to be human."

### Three Messaging Pillars

**Pillar 1 — Execution over conversation**
> Most AI tools help customers find answers. Alltius agents approve loans, process claims, and close insurance policies. The difference is what happens at the end of the conversation.

**Pillar 2 — Built by people who understand AI at the deepest level**
> Vibs's IIT Kanpur + Wharton PhD + CMU + UCI background, combined with teaching NLP, means Alltius is not guessing at what works. This is AI built by people who understand it from first principles.

**Pillar 3 — Financial services is not a use case — it's the only case**
> Alltius is not a horizontal tool retrofitted for finance. It was built from day one for the compliance requirements, legacy system integrations, and regulatory constraints that make financial services uniquely hard.

- Product pitches in Month 1
